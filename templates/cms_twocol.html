Floodlight is about two things: storytelling and positive change.
Our goal is to help you use the first one to achieve the second.

Welcome to Floodlight—a, community-powered storytelling project designed to drive social change by giving individuals and organizations the skills and tools to generate visually-compelling, data-supported stories that inspire people to act and illuminate community issues.

{% placeholder page-pull-1-title or %}The Vision{% endplaceholder %}

{% placeholder page-pull-1 or %}

We can’t change what we can’t see. But together our stories can illuminate issues that matter and brighten the world.

We believe in the inherent power, desire and creativity of people to enlighten the others in their community about the critical issues that affect us all. We also believe that thriving communities hinge on full citizen engagement.

Floodlight strives to empower as many community members as possible to translate their own firsthand, on-the-ground experiences and knowledge into multi-media and data-rich stories to that can direct action to improve people’s lives.

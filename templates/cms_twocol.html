  • {% placeholder large-copy-3 or %} Together, We’re Telling A Bigger Story {% endplaceholder %}

Floodlight will not only give you a powerful platform to get your voice heard on issues that matter to you, but it will alsoadd your voice to an emerging chorus of community storytellers and change makers, igniting interest around issues that matter to each of us.

    In this way, Floodlight becomes a unique nexus between storytellers, story sharers and story readers each working together to brighten, inform and transform community though our collective stories.

{% placeholder mini-post-1-title or %}The Vision{% endplaceholder %}

{% placeholder mini-post-1 or %}

We can’t change what we can’t see. But together our stories can illuminate issues that matter and brighten the world.

We believe in the inherent power, desire and creativity of people to enlighten the others in their community about the critical issues that affect us all. We also believe that thriving communities hinge on full citizen engagement.

Floodlight strives to empower as many community members as possible to translate their own firsthand, on-the-ground experiences and knowledge into multi-media and data-rich stories to that can direct action to improve people’s lives.

Created By Impassioned Storytellers, Data Geeks and Community Change Agents.

Floodlight was created by a team of impassioned social change agents, investigative journalists, data visualization experts and technology gurus with the goal of helping community members create and share inspiring, data-driven stories. Through the use of a free, open source platform, Floodlight helps residents illuminate their own issues while contributing to a larger unfolding story about our community.
